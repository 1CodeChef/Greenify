About Us

Here at Greenify we are motivated by the importance of sustainability and green living. Our small team of designers strive to produce and sell clothing which has as little impact to our world as possible. Coming from all different types of backgrounds, we are able to contribute new and innovative ideas and come together to make what we think the world so desperately needs.


All of our garments are organically sourced with organic agricultural standards, we push for all our suppliers to be paid fair wages and have clean and comfortable working conditions. Wherever possible, we want to use second hand and recycled materials as much as we possibly can. From our cotton being organically sourced to our jeans which are produced with 70% less water consumption to the average, we push ourselves to provide sustainable alternatives. Our clothes are designed to be long lasting essential pieces to your wardrobe, with hopes of discouraging our customers from investing into fast fashion as frequently.


Although we are dedicated to help educate the world on eco fashion, we are far from perfect. This step towards clean living is a constant learning experience to us all. If you are interested in joining us alongside this experience, feel free to subscribe to our newsletter below!
